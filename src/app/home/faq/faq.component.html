Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I raise a ticket?

Click on the “Raise Ticket” option from the main menu, fill out the required details, and submit. A ticket ID will be generated.

2. How can I track my ticket status?

Navigate to the “My Tickets” section. You will see a list of your submitted tickets along with their current status.

3. Can I edit or delete a ticket once it's submitted?

Tickets can only be edited or closed by support staff. However, you can add comments or update relevant details from the ticket view page.

4. Who handles my ticket after submission?

Based on the category you choose, your ticket will be assigned to the relevant team or support personnel.

5. What if I don’t get a response in time?

You can escalate your ticket by clicking the “Escalate” button in your ticket view page. It will notify a higher support tier.
